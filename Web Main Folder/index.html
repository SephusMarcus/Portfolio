ABOUT
My name is Marc Joseph D. Gordillo, I'm a graphic artist student and an aspiring digital artist.

At a very young age, I was already passionate in art and one of the main reasons was that I grew up watching animated films and was inspired to create art. Years pass by I begun to pursue art as a career and became knowledgeable in art as well as improving my skills as well.

Today, I am still constantly inspired by the films I watched but also some other form of art as well such as comics, manga and video games.
